VIRTUAL PLEASURE - 病理学的思考 cd release!
Posted in 12/28/21 - 16:04 by Euroblitz

i've started into vaporwave world since i was 12-14yo, i really fell in love with it. now i started to sample some tunes here and there, mostly barber beats or something that i like to call 'post-vapor-trash-like-wave-something'. odd, huh? yeah, for now they are simply and neatly arranged samples, but that doesn't takes off the credit from me.

my band/artistc name is called VIRTUAL PLEASURE on bandcamp, and it has a page too, you can check it out. my last two works was two ~30min long eps with 4 or 5 songs on each, just to name a few of them. they're usually composed of slow jazz tunes that arrange neatly looped to create amazing barber beats, just like some vaporwave artists i follow and love do them (macroblank, haircuts for men, and so on).

a quick photo of me taping some details on the album cover, that gonna be released soon (i hope)

you can check the album on bandcamp in this link. i did choose these mini 8cm cds/single because they're neat for a small-lenght album or few songs. i often did see them shipping with cheap usb controllers or webcam for providing drivers, as they only can store less than 200mb in data and 21-23 minutes of raw audio for cd players. i've finished the casing design and almost finishing the cd face sticker, the cds i bought are yellow with name labels to write on them, a printable sticker should do the trick.

i'm going to start selling them soon on bandcamp, starting 10 USD base price for the clear plastic shells, as each of the copies are personalised, and 5 USD for the simple paper/carton sleeve covers. both of them comes with the same number of tracks, but i had to shorten a track or two to make them fit on these cds, not a big deal, just put repeat on. and the 10 USD version comes with a custom... thing on the plastic shell, hehe. just stay tuned on my bandcamp and/or twitter to check how's going.
