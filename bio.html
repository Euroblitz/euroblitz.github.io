About me

Well, what can I say? I'm a Linux enthustiast of course. I like to shove down every part of my PC and operating system since I was 7-8 (my 'hobby' when I was at that age was looking through windows/system32 folders and clicking on each of the .exes in there, just to check what the heck each of them do). Later when I was 10-12 I've started to read some old books about Visual Basic 6.0 and creating some programs for fun, and that's when I also started with GNU+Linux.

Euro/Euroblitz is my nickname and my internet name, so I prefer to leave things like this. I'm 25 now, born and living in Brazil, almost fluent in english and self learnt, and I have a boyfriend, we're together since mid 2019 and it's the best thing that happened to me on this decade. I'm a calm guy, I get better sinergy with computers and electronics than people sometimes, but this doesn't prevents me from having a good time and conversation with people. I'm also a grade 1 autistic person, this means I'm not that good at talking, taking facial expressions, sometimes I get a little clunky at work when I'm on that days to talk with my coworkers. I'm too concentrated in odd things, sometimes I dropped my classes just for watching some coding videos and trying to replicate them in java, that's just not too good sometimes. But I try to get things done as far as I can.

What I currently do
